Description

Welcome to Rainbowcore Hypernova, the explosively fun and absurd fighting game experience!

  • Choose from seven vibrant and colorful characters, each representing a different color of the rainbow.
  • Master the easy-to-learn controls with just three buttons and a variety of motion inputs.
  • Engage in fast-paced and dynamic battles with a unique arsenal of moves for every character.
  • Immerse yourself in a banger soundtrack that blends epic music with absurd mouth noises.
  • Explosive visuals set against the backdrop of a surreal underground mushroom cave.
  • Connect with other players seamlessly through peer-to-peer rollback netcode gameplay.
  • Best of all, Rainbowcore Hypernova is completely free to play!

Join the Rainbowcore Hypernova community and unleash your inner fighter. Show off your skills and become the ultimate Rainbowcore Hypernova champion!
