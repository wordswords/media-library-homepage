Description

The family was ready to visit the amusement park, but plans are cancelled due to weather.
This is a story about an ordinary family spending time at home during the rain.

"Rainy Season" is a narrative game that is playable to finish in less than 1 hour.


  • Interact with objects
Touch and interact with items within house to learn about them.


  • Talk to the family
During various times, listen and engage in conversations with the family.


  • Daydreams
Special events will occur when bored.
