Description

Banzai Escape 2 Subterranean is a sequel game based on the events of Banzai Escape 2 where the Draugr labs have been captured by SPECTRE. All of the sisters (Inchester, Imingter and Iekler) take in refugees to a location deep underground. In order to survive, they must defeat the Lanun occupying the underground dungeon networks along with other factions such as SPECTRE and others

You are an Elite Imingter agent with special access to roaming around underground networks to dispose any incoming threat.
