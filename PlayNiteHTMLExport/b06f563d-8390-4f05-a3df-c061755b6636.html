Description

Resurrected from extinction on a remote island chain, dinosaurs have escaped to wreak chaos! An elite mercenary team is now sent to eliminate the primal threat...

ROAR POWER!


Take control of 12 deadly dinosaur classes, from sneaky Spitters to towering Tyrants, all of them ruthless and very hungry. Each species has unique traits and attacks, so unleash your Roar Ability to gain a tactical advantage!

NOT JUST A WALK IN THE PARK.


The mercenary team uses an arsenal of devastating weaponry and gadgets to send these bloodthirsty beasts back to the Cretaceous. Select from five distinct human classes, each with their own special utilities. Watch your back, or you're extinct!

CHOOSE YOUR MISSION


  • Fight to the top of the food chain in all out prehistoric warfare!
  • Work together to reach rescue, or ensure the humans never leave the island!
  • Go challenge yourself to a never-ending onslaught of AI dinosaurs in co-op.
  • Stalk the humans as an invisible raptor, or band together to take it down.
  • Steal eggs from dinosaur nests or protect your unhatched babies!

65 MILLION YEARS OF FASHION


Deck out your dinos with model mutations, bling out your guns, or drive your enemies mad with taunts! Unlock skins and more as free drops, buy or sell on Steam Marketplace, and earn Extinction Points (EXP) to rank up and unlock cosmetics.

Submit new items on our Steam Workshop for a chance of them being officially added!

BIGGER. BETTER. MORE TEETH.


Featuring NEW species, gametypes, weapons, maps, and MORE, Primal Carnage: Extinction takes dinosaur combat to the next level! And we're not done yet, so be sure to check out the Official Discord Server!
