Description



Build your own maps or pick from a selection of ready-made ones.

Place up to seven opposing armies made of different types of beasts and watch them tear each other apart in an epic battle! Or join the fight yourself in the first-person mode and blow the enemy away with some powerful guns!

You will have a huge selection of more than 70 creatures to pick from and new ones are added every two weeks!



Create your own custom monsters by combining different body parts.

Add weapons to your custom creatures!

Ultimate flexibility. You can attach as many body parts and weapons as you want, anywhere you want!


You can download a vast selection of custom monsters, maps, and buildings created by other players from the Steam Workshop. Endless fun awaits you!

You can also upload your own creations for other people to try out.


Test your tactical and strategic expertise in the campaign mode. Pick the right beasts, place them in the right place, and command them to defeat the enemy.

The most expensive army doesn't always win. Units have strengths and weaknesses. Find out what they are and use that knowledge to your advantage on the battlefield!
