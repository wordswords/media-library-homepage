Description

This game requires a VR headset in order to play and is available in Polish language only.



 

DISCOVER THE HIDDEN PRINTING HOUSE OF FIGHTING SOLIDARITY AND IMMERSE YOURSELF IN THE SECRETS OF 80s RESISTANCE

“Szybowcowa '87” transports players back to the 1980s, right into a secret printing house of Fighting Solidarity located in an apartment on Szybowcowa Street in Wrocław's Gądów district. This Virtual Reality experience allows you to explore the place where the members of the Fighting Solidarity movement produced illegal press materials in their struggle against the communist regime. With authentic resources digitized from original documents, players can immerse themselves in the atmosphere of the resistance movement, and actively participate in the events that transpired during this tumultuous period.

Fighting Solidarity, a Polish anti-communist underground organization founded in Wrocław, gathered hundreds of members and thousands of supporters. It was in the printing house on Szybowcowa where underground publications were produced on semitransparent blotting paper, which was prohibited by the authorities.

 

Key Features:

A story set in the realities of the 1980s, reflecting the activities of Fighting Solidarity and the struggles of the resistance

Gameplay that combines elements of exploration, the production of clandestine press, and listening to intercepted communications from the opposition.

A unique opportunity to discover the historical location where underground press materials were created

Intricately detailed artifacts and documents sourced from real archival materials, providing a deeper understanding of life in the People's Republic of Poland
