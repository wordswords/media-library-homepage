Description

An Ever-changing World

In Svarog's Dream, the absolute freedom to explore the world and make decisions is yours. However, every action influences the world around you, for better or worse.

A Soul Lost Between Worlds

You start as a lost soul, inhabiting human hosts who are desperate enough to embrace you. With each death, you revisit the underworld to possess a new body from a selection of unique characters.

A Pawn in the Game of Gods

While humans wage their wars on Earth, the Gods clash against each other in the sky.

Even with their immense power, they search for a means to gain momentum in their eternal battles. All existence is bound by the threads of destiny.

Except for you.

You have the power of free will, a gift the gods will go to great lengths to persuade to their side.

The old prophecy:

"Svarog, the first god, created six worlds and fell asleep.

In his dream, he created the seventh world, Earth.

Once he wakes up, Earth will cease to exist."

Immersive Gameplay

Embrace a living, responsive world filled with emergent stories, while also experiencing over 100 handcrafted quests.

Eat, cook, sleep, craft potions, upgrade gear, buy houses, take loans, and even evade debt collectors or headhunters, among much more.

Create your own character build using abilities from 12 unique classes, and equip them with hundreds of weapons, armor pieces, and accessories.

"We took everything we loved about old-school RPGs and then added a modern twist on top of it all."
