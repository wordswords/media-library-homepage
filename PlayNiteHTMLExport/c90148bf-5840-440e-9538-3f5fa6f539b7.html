Description

This collection includes four parts：

1, Commando 2

The game has been fully optimized in this compilation, with new enemies added, enriched level design and gameplay mechanics, high-definition images, balanced difficulty, and adjusted AI for some bosses. Additionally, many interesting sound effects have been added, making the game experience more vivid for players.

After entering the game, you can find a wealth of customizable options in the settings screen in the upper left corner, such as enemy generation multiplier, boss summoning troops and blood effect personalization. Through these settings, you will experience a different kind of gameplay from your childhood, and you will be able to face the tidal wave of enemies and feel the extreme pleasure of sweeping away thousands of troops!


2, Commando X Allied Assault

As an offshoot of Commando 2, players in this game will engage in positional confrontation with enemy forces, experiencing a completely different group combat mode from that of Generation 2. As with "Commando 2", this work has also been fully optimized to bring a better gaming experience.

3, Commando 3

As a sequel to Commando 2, this game adds a rich mount system, more enemy types and battle scenes, while the game's painting style has changed from a Q version to a more realistic style, bringing players a more realistic war experience.

4, Special Gift Section

This section contains a small music video for Commando 2, scanned designs, a curtain call animation for the entire cast, and an introduction to the series' future plans. This special content provides players with even more to enjoy and look forward to outside of the game.

Whether you're a veteran or a newcomer to the series, Commando Collection will bring you unprecedentedly intense battles and a rich gaming experience. Get ready to step onto the battlefields of World War II and experience the passion of combat!
