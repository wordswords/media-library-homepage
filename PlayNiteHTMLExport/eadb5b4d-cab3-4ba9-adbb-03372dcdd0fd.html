Description

Brutal, Fast, and Free

Alien Grounds is a no-nonsense, fast-paced FPS where the difficulty ramps up fast.
It starts easy. Then it gets personal... and addictive.

  • Hard mode that earns its name – no tutorials, no mercy 

  • Easy mode available – for training or casual play

  • Every shot counts – smart aiming = higher scores

  • Maps shift mid-battle – no two runs feel the same

  • Steam achievements – brutal challenges only a few will ever earn

  • Instant action – no cutscenes, no waiting, just fight

You’ll die. You’ll retry. You’ll get better.
And if you make it to Mission 100 on Hard… you’ve earned bragging rights.

Weapons

🔫 Pistol – Low damage, limited ammo. Best used for headshots. Starts in Mission #1.

💥Automatic Rifle – Recoil, bullet spread, and a scope. Ammo spawns when low. Unlocks in Mission #2.

⚡ Laser Gun – No recoil, infinite energy, scoped – but it overheats with sustained fire. Unlocks in Mission #3.

🌀 Gravity Gun – No recoil, infinite energy, scoped – but it speeds up time. Unlocks in Mission #4.

☢️ Annihilator – Annihilates nearby enemies. Short recharge delay. Activate with middle mouse button. Unlocks in Mission #5.

Just play it!

No microtransactions. No paywalls. Just a solo dev making a game that's fun, fair, and deadly.
Hit Play and see how far you can go.


Technical Notes

  • Runs on slower computers – tweak settings for better performance

  • Gamepad friendly – raw input, aim assist, and sensitivity options

  • Y-Axis Invert – works for both mouse and gamepad

  • Left-handed keyboard and mouse setup supported

  • Steam Deck tested
