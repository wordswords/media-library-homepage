Horn of the Abyss


The definitive fan expansion to Heroes of Might and Magic III.

Horn of the Abyss (HotA) is a meticulously crafted fan-made expansion for Heroes of Might and Magic III, designed to seamlessly integrate with the original game's mechanics and aesthetics. Developed by HotA Crew, this expansion introduces new content that honors the legacy of the original while enhancing gameplay depth and variety.
 

New Factions: Cove and Factory


Cove
Set sail with the Cove, a seafaring faction hailing from the pirate kingdom of Regna. This town introduces a lineup of aquatic and pirate-themed creatures, such as Nymphs, Corsairs, and the terrifying Sea Serpents. The Cove's distinctive gameplay revolves around naval dominance and swift assaults, offering a fresh strategic experience.

Factory
Embrace innovation with the Factory, a steampunk-inspired faction blending technology and nature. Its creatures range from Halflings and Armadillos to mechanical Automatons and the powerful Dreadnoughts. The Factory's unique dual-path development allows players to choose between technological advancement and natural synergy, providing diverse tactical options.

Expanded Hero Roster


HotA enriches the hero lineup by adding 16 new heroes for each of the new towns, Cove and Factory, as well as a number of striking campaign-specific figures, totaling 37 new playable characters. Additionally, several existing heroes have been reintroduced or rebalanced to enhance gameplay diversity.

New Campaigns


HotA introduces five new campaigns, expanding the game's narrative and strategic depth:

  • Under the Jolly Roger: Follow Captain Jeremy Albatross on his quest for survival as he finds himself in uncharted land.  
  • Terror of the Seas: Navigate treacherous waters and confront formidable foes in this suspenseful maritime adventure.  
  • Horn of the Abyss: Delve into the mystery surrounding the eponymous great artifact and its impact on the world.  
  • Forged in Fire: Experience the rise of the Factory faction amidst the chaos of wars and find the best use for your heroes’ ingenious innovations.  
  • All In: Witness your former foe embarking on a high-stakes journey that tests the limits of strategy and courage.  

These campaigns provide hours of immersive gameplay.

Revamped and Expanded


  • Dozens of new artifacts, including the devastating Horn of the Abyss and the mind-shielding Sleepkeeper, change the flow of combat and map control.
  • Some secondary skills are revamped with the aim to make each one playable and useful without crossing into overpowered territory.
  • Classic characters return with fresh polish, and familiar faces reemerge with lore-respecting twists.
  • Enhanced map and campaign editors, new terrains like Highlands and Wasteland, a wealth of new objects and options, as well as refined random map templates, give creators more power than ever.
  • Updated visuals, interface enhancements, and subtle town balance changes make everything smoother, sharper, and more satisfying.

From external dwellings accumulating weekly growths to the all-new spell research system, Horn of the Abyss is designed with convenience, mechanical depth and immersive lore in mind. Every change respects the source material while pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the Heroes III universe.

Whether you're chasing nostalgic glory or discovering this legend for the first time, Horn of the Abyss is the definitive way to play one of the greatest turn-based strategy games ever made.
