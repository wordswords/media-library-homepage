Description

Defend your lane


Endless waves of enemies threaten your king. Assemble your army from over 100 unique fighters, each with its own strengths and abilities. Empower your defenses with auras and spells, and change the tides of battle by unleashing powerful mercenary attacks. How many waves can you last before your defenses collapse and your king falls? Outwit and break your opponents before they break you.

Choose your playstyle


Build defensively to hold the line against swarms of enemies, play offensively to smash through your opponents' fighters, or prioritize economy to scale into the late game and become an unbreakable juggernaut. Every game requires adaptation, but play to your strengths to achieve victory.

Master your legions


Choose from 8 unique legions and over 100 fighters. Select fighters from each legion for 12 million possible combinations. Some fighters secure you strategic advantages in the early game. Others facilitate powerful late game synergies.

Core Features

  • Party as 1-8 players. Play 2v2 and 4v4 matchmade games.
  • Single-player/co-op campaign.* (~8-12 hours) and 10 levels of AI's.
  • Friendly & active playerbase. Fast, fair matches. Since 2017, still going strong in March 2024!
  • 10+ years of development. Thousands of hours have gone into refining the core game loop.

Highlights

  • Enjoyable to learn. Efficient 15-min tutorial. In-game companion ("Game Coach") gives you tips as you play. Co-op campaign is beatable for players of all skill levels. New-player friendly matchmaking eases you into PvP.
  • Infinite replayability. No two games are the same. Even after 4,000 hours, the game is still fresh.
  • Best-in-the-world game balance. Driven by both data and feedback to ensure strategic variety and competitive fairness.
  • Stats for days. Ranked seasons & rewards, profiles filled with graphs & tables, full match histories, and leaderboards.
  • eSports. $750 cash tournaments every month.
  • No pay to win. In-game purchases are 100% cosmetic.
  • Global servers. All multiplayer is cross-region with no lag.
  • FairPlay detection system. AFKers, griefers, and toxic players are dealt with quickly.
  • Weekly challenges. A puzzle-like competition to find the best build with a set lineup.

*Note: single-player features require an online connection due to technical limitations of the game engine.

History

Legion TD 2 is the sequel to the 2nd most popular Warcraft 3 mod of all time, Legion TD. Also known as Squadron TD in Starcraft 2. The top 5 most played Warcraft 3 mods are DotA, Legion TD, Wintermaul, Footmen Frenzy, and Life of a Peasant. Legion TD is proud to hold the silver medal.




Legion TD 2 launched in early access in 2017, and has received major updates, quality of life, and balance changes every single month since then. After fully launching in October 2021, we believe it has grown to become the most replayable, balanced, competitive strategy experience out there. Unlike other indie multiplayer games, Legion TD 2's playerbase has stood the test of time, having been active & steady for over 6 years and counting.
