Description



Play the drums in virtual reality like a Rock Hero, and survive in hell fighting against demons and their boss!



Features:

  • Custom songs and Workshop support! Unleash your creativity and take your gameplay to a whole new level.
  • Discover optional paid DLC tracks feat. Green Day, The Offspring, Undertale, Simple Plan, HammerFall and more!
  • Real Rock! Hard rock, heavy metal, Nu metal, among others. A wide variety of rock, with original music or hit groups!
  • Stylish and crazy graphics, with a brutal finishing level
  • TRUE Drums Set! A drums set that you will have to play and you can customize!
  • More than 5h of only CAMPAIGN! Plus all the extras!
  • Dozens of unlockables! Drumsticks, drums, songs...
  • Intuitive gameplay for everyone. No dizziness. Play in a chair.
  • Feel the rock n roll with advanced levels, make flourishes with the sticks and master the drums.
  • PHASE enemies and boss, special levels and different worlds to discover.
  • Online Ranking! Compete against your friends and beat their scores.
  • Musical versions of other videogames!! Watch out!
  • 4 levels of difficulty! For all levels and with a fair progression!
  • More than 33 songs and new songs are incoming!
  • With classic songs like "I Love Rock 'N Roll" and "Drink" by ALESTORM and more!
  • Spectacular boss battles!
